Land and Sea Adventures:

Tours in Iceland

Whale watching tours

Whale-watching tours give you the chance to see incredible marine animals up close in their natural habitat. As you sail across the open sea, you might spot majestic humpback whales or orcas, and if you're lucky, you may even witness them performing special moves like tail-slapping, creating an unforgettable and thrilling adventure that you’ll remember for a long time specially if you do it during the winter when there is northern lights.

Tours in Iceland

ATV Tours in Reykjavik

ATV tours offer an exciting and adventurous way to explore Iceland’s wild and rugged landscapes. You’ll ride through volcanic terrain, across vast lava fields, and possibly even along black sand beaches, experiencing both the thrill of off-roading and the breathtaking beauty of the scenery surrounding you, making it a truly memorable outdoor experience.

Tours in Iceland

Hot Springs of Iceland

Hot spring tours offer a relaxing and unique way to experience Iceland’s natural wonders. You can soak in warm, geothermal waters surrounded by stunning landscapes, from rugged mountains to vast lava fields. Some tours take you to famous spots like the Blue Lagoon, while others bring you to more hidden, natural pools where you can unwind in peace. The soothing waters, rich in minerals, are not only great for relaxing but also good for your skin. Whether you’re looking for a quiet retreat or a scenic adventure, hot spring tours provide a perfect balance of relaxation and natural beauty.
