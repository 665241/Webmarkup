Skógafoss Waterfall:

Attractions in Iceland

Exploring the Skógafoss Waterfall

Skógafoss Waterfall is a very attractive spot where you can hike on a trail that starts at Skógafoss Waterfall and follows the Skóga river upstream for 8 kilometers. The best time to undertake this hike is from June through September, when the trail is typically free of snow and easily accessible.

Walking along Skógá River

As you hike through this stunningly beautiful green landscape, you will encounter not just one, but 25 additional waterfalls cascading down the mountains, as well as majestic glacier-capped volcanoes rising dramatically in the distance. This breathtaking scenery creates a magical atmosphere, making each step of your journey a delightful surprise filled with natural wonders and picturesque views.

Attractions in Iceland

When to visit

If you’re looking to experience Skógafoss Waterfall all by yourself, we recommend arriving very early in the morning or later in the day to avoid the crowds. This stunning waterfall is a very popular tourist attraction, and even during those times, you may not find it completely empty, especially during the high season. However, if you’re traveling in late autumn, consider visiting after dark, when the northern lights dance across the sky, adding an enchanting touch to the experience and making it even more magical.
